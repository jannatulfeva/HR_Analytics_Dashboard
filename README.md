# HR_Analytics_Dashboard

# Overview

- An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard designed to analyze employee data and uncover insights related to attrition and workforce trends

- Focuses on identifying patterns in employee turnover and key influencing factors

- Helps support data-driven HR decision-making through visual insights

# Business Objective

- Analyze employee attrition patterns

- Identify factors contributing to employee turnover

- Understand workforce demographics (age, salary, job role, etc.)

- Provide actionable insights for improving employee retention

# Tools & Technologies

- Power BI – Dashboard creation & data visualization

- Excel – Data cleaning & preprocessing

# Key Features

KPI Metrics:

- Total Employees

- Attrition Count

- Attrition Rate

- Average Age, Salary, and Years at Company

Attrition Analysis by:

- Gender

- Age Group

- Education Field

- Salary Range

- Job Role

- Years at Company

# Key Insights

- Highest attrition observed among employees aged 26–35

- Employees in lower salary ranges show higher turnover

- Certain job roles (e.g., Sales Executive, Laboratory Technician) have higher attrition

- Majority of employees belong to specific education fields such as Life Sciences

- Attrition trends vary based on years at the company
